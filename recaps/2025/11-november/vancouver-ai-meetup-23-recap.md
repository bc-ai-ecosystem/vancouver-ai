## 3. WhatsApp AI: Energy Monitoring & Token Savings Award

### Lionel Emba's Presentation

**WhatsApp for Open Router:**
- Scrapes Open Router leaderboard (no API available)
- Integrates EcoLogits for carbon/water estimation
- Shows monthly gigawatts used, CO2 emissions, projected water usage
- Includes equivalency conversions (km in car, microwave hours, etc.)

**Google's Energy Report:**
- First holistic methodology published
- **0.24 watt hour per prompt**
- **5 drops of water per prompt**
- Problem: Per-prompt not comparable across providers (tokens are real unit)

**Open Source Under Attack:**
- **curl project:** 8x more vulnerability reports, only 5% legitimate (AI hallucinating bounties)
- **FFMPEG:** Google Big Sleep 90-day disclosure policy overwhelming underfunded maintainers
- Open source enables all transparency: "There would be no data on this matter otherwise"

**1 Trillion Token Equivalency:**
- 52,000 households daily energy use
- 3.5 million km driven in a car

### Token Savings Award Proposal

> "What if we turn this onto its head and actually created an award for saving tokens?"

**Concept:**
- Reward efficiency, not consumption
- Share methods that save 1M/10M/100M tokens
- Document and share findings with community
- "Not using less of AI, but make a more sober use of it"

**Business Logic:**
- "If you want to change the outcomes, you change the incentives"
- OpenAI's award gamifies consumption
- BC + AI could award efficiency instead

**Q&A Highlights:**
- Energy mix configurable in WhatsApp for Open Router
- Default: world electricity mix, can simulate by region
- Discussion on open vs closed AI privacy implications

## 4. AI Voice Rights: Amanda Silvera's Story & SOBIR

### The Violation

Amanda Silvera, voice of Psych to Go (12.9M YouTube subscribers), discovered her voice was cloned:

**Most Disturbing Case:**
> "A gentleman who was using my voice to pose as a young girl and lure older men in, posing as this young girl. And it was all live videos of these men. And my voice was being used to seduce them."

**Ongoing Issues:**
- Reddit threads sharing "Amanda Silvera voice model"
- DMCA takedowns ineffective (one down, five pop up)
- Clones behind paywalls and in private spaces
- Potential biometric security implications (voice banking)

### Legal Gray Area

> "I was hitting a wall when it came to my rights. I was hitting a wall when it came to how much of my voice is even mine."

- Even lawyers frustrated by unclear jurisdiction
- Extends to all biometric data: gait, likeness, corneas, retinas

### SOBIR Founded

**Society for Original Biometric Identity Rights:**
- Pushing for policy changes and legislature
- change.org petition to Minister Evan Solomon (5,300+ signatures)
- Seeking Lead Petition platform for government attention

**Key Insight:**
> "It's not AI that's doing this. It's people. It's people abusing something."

### Tech Solution Concept

Amanda seeking team for biometric protection technology:
- Senior ML engineers (voice/audio)
- Deepfake detection engineers
- Synthetic media identification
- Watermarking specialists
- Cloud architects (data sovereignty)
- Biometric cybersecurity engineers
- Policy and compliance technologists

### Connection Made

Sesson Ong (government relations) offered to connect Amanda with:
- BC + AI parliamentary secretary
- Lead Petition platform
- Policy maker engagement strategy

## 5. Ethics Panel: "Portals Not Panels" Deep Dive

### Panelists

| **Kay Tigo** | Director of Marketing | Montreal AI Ethics Institute |

### Catherine Warren - Context Setting

**Background:**
- Former CEO, Vancouver Economic Commission
- Climate physics background
- Launched Edmonton Ethical AI Accelerator Fund
- 25 years at FanTrust (innovation/entertainment/economic development)

**Key Framing:**
- AI as "double-edged sword, or maybe even an AK-47"
- "We don't need intelligence to address the climate emergency... This is not an intelligence problem. It's a problem of political will."
- "Can artificial intelligence fix human idiocy?"

**Vancouver's Position:**
- Global powerhouse in media and entertainment
- Home of Adbusters, Greenpeace
- Global center for circularity and B Corp movement

### Kay Tigo - State of AI Ethics Report

**Report Stats:**
- 58 contributors
- 48 essays
- 17 chapters
- Covers: sustainability, healthcare, military, entertainment
- Free download: montreal.ethics.ai

**Edward Wilson Quote:**
> "The problem with humanity is that we have paleolithic emotions with medieval institutions and godlike technology."

**Key Statistics:**
- 5-second AI video = 3.4 million joules = 61 km e-bike = 1+ hour microwave
- By 2028: 326 TWh/year for AI = some countries' entire usage
- 326 TWh = 300 billion miles driven = 1,600 round trips Earth to Sun
- 1.5Â°C exceeded = 1 billion people facing water stress/desertification
- 14% of world's species at extinction risk
- Coral reefs decline 70-90%

**Core Argument:**
> "AI is built on theft."

**On Corporate Responsibility:**
> "Have corporations ever been intrinsically motivated to uphold human rights? Policy is one of the biggest ways to get corporations to actually do the right thing."

### Kevin Friel - Compute & GPU Monopoly

**The Problem:**
- Nvidia's 20-year lead on hardware/software
- $5 trillion elephant in the room
- Only game in town for GPU compute at scale

**Needed Revolution:**
- ARM-style disruption for GPU (like Apple Silicon did for CPU)
- Nvidia 4090: 500W vs Apple Silicon equivalent: 40W
- Canadian opportunity: Nortel, TeleSat, Blackberry precedent

**Proposed Solution: Compute Carbon Offset**
- Tax credit incentive for efficient computing reports
- Fiscal accountability for model choices
- "Fix it in pre" philosophy for compute

**Personal Context:**
- Wasn't working for 2 years before AI pivot
- "It came into my industry and kicked the fucking table over"

### Liz Marshall - Water Justice & New Documentary

**Previous Work:**
- Water on the Table (2010) - following Maude Barlow at UN
- Meat the Future (2020) - cultivated meat documentary

**New Documentary:**
> "Is AI draining this world for another?"

**Statistics:**
- By 2027: Data centers = 1.7 trillion gallons water/year
- Canada: 20% world's freshwater, only 7% renewable
- 36 Indigenous nations still without potable water

**CASK Framework:**
- **C**uriosity
- **A**wareness
- **S**kepticism
- **C**aution

**Resistance Models:**
- Georgia, Indianapolis, Virginia: moratoriums on new data centers
- "The resistance has arrived"
- "People have power. We have to remember that."

> "Design a new economic system... This is not a technology problem. It's not even a political problem, it's an economic problem."
