## Technical Deep Dives

### Lionel Ring's WhatsApp Energy Monitoring Project

**The Problem:**
- AI is ubiquitous and energy-intensive but has zero reporting on energy usage
- 55% of AI requests in coding environments are never used (wasted due to UX patterns)  
- Energy demand likely going parabolic without measurement tools

**The Solution:**
- Built Visual Studio Code extension measuring real-time AI energy usage
- Created dashboard showing hourly/daily/monthly analytics  
- Used "microwave hours" as relatable energy metric
- Made open-source and available on GitHub

**Shocking Results:**
- One development session = running microwave for 20 hours nonstop
- AI auto-complete requests on every keystroke create massive waste
- Corporate transparency deliberately obscured to hide scale of consumption

**Industry Context:**
Trump administration removed environmental review requirements for AI data centers, indicating the scale of energy infrastructure being built without oversight.

### Michael's AI Development Mastery

**Background:** Built 52 startups in 52 weeks to find product-market fit, eventually focusing on AudioWave (text-to-audio).

**Key Philosophy:**

- Customers don't care about your team size, they want solutions
- Keep it simple vs. extend leverage (balance between capability and complexity)

**Advanced Workflows:**
