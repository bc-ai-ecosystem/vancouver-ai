### **Kush - "Demystifying Large Language Models"**
**The Technical Deep-Dive That Brought the Heat**

Kush delivered what Kris called "bringing the freaking heat" - a comprehensive technical breakdown of how language models actually work:

- **Core Insight:** LLMs are fundamentally "compressed internet" - massive datasets compressed into gigabyte-sized files
- **Token Prediction Reality:** Models only predict one token at a time, creating an illusion of intelligence through autoregressive generation
- **Live Demonstrations:** Real-time probability distributions showing how models choose next words
- **Safety & Control Issues:** Demonstrated how fine-tuned safety measures can be bypassed through prompt engineering
- **Political Perspective:** Argued that AI safety concerns are being weaponized by tech giants to maintain centralized control and prevent open source adoption

**Key Quote:** *"These models are not thinking - they are just giving next word prediction. To anthropomorphize them feeds into the propaganda of Sam Altman and OpenAI."*

### **Michael & Caroline Roddy Wolf - Indigenous AI Education Pipeline**
**Building Computer Science Access in Indigenous Communities**

The husband-and-wife team shared their groundbreaking work addressing massive underrepresentation in AI:

- **Stark Statistics:** Only 1 Indigenous PhD graduate in computer science across all of North America (Canada, US, Mexico) out of 1,700 total graduates
- **Failed Traditional Approaches:** Despite programs from Amazon/Google, not a single Indigenous student completed their recruited programs
- **Innovative Solution:** Created intensive summer camps teaching Lakota youth in rural South Dakota
- **Proven Results:** Every sophomore/junior/senior who completed their 3-year program is now pursuing computer science degrees
- **Partnership with Mila:** Working with Montreal's AI research institute on Indigenous AI pathways

**AGI as "Cargo Cult":** Michael introduced the powerful concept that AGI expectations mirror cargo cults - communities building replicas of technology they don't control, hoping to attract its benefits.

### **Amin Sharifi - "AI as the Philosopher's Stone"**
**Indigenous Knowledge Systems & Universal Responsibility**

Amin delivered a profound philosophical framework connecting AI to indigenous wisdom:

- **Alchemy Metaphor:** AI as the philosopher's stone capable of turning "lead" (colonial systems) into "gold" (sustainable, reciprocal relationships)
- **Global Context:** Only 700 million people worldwide have interacted with AI; Vancouver's AI community has rare access and responsibility
- **Indigenous Integration:** Traditional Ecological Knowledge (TEK) offers frameworks for sustainable AI development
- **Four Pillars for Ethical AI:**
  1. Inclusive development (include Indigenous voices)
  2. Data sovereignty (protect community data)
  3. Education and access (remove barriers)
  4. Environmental stewardship (tech + TEK = gold)

**Key Quote:** *"All of you are now alchemists, whether you want it or not. You can create more lead and more shit in this shitty world, or you can start creating gold."*
