## üåü Standout Moments & Presentations

### **Patrick Penfather - Interactive "Gifts and Coal" Christmas Performance**
**The Community Researcher's AI Ethics Survey**

Patrick delivered a brilliant musical performance that doubled as legitimate research, dividing the audience into two singing groups while conducting an ethics survey about AI usage:

- **Survey Findings:** High adoption rates, low EULA reading, organizational AI rule-breaking
- **Musical Innovation:** "Coals in your stocking, gifts under the tree" became a participatory ethics examination
- **Research Impact:** Using the community as a case study for global AI adoption patterns
- **Birthday Tribute:** Led the entire room in harmonized happy birthday singing

### **Kevin Friel (Pixel Wizard) - Real-Time Generative Backgrounds**
**Hollywood Production Values Made Accessible**

Kevin's technical demonstration showcased the democratization of high-end broadcast technology:

- **Technical Specs:** Stable Diffusion Turbo on NVIDIA 4090 running at 14fps
- **Innovation:** Real-time podcast background generation responsive to conversation
- **Cost Disruption:** $25,000 broadcast systems replaced by $5,000 open source solutions
- **Professional Context:** Former Global/CTV graphics producer bringing broadcast expertise to podcasting

### **Carol Anne Hilton - Indigenomics AI Vision**
**Global Indigenous Economic Reconciliation Movement**

Caroline presented her audacious vision for indigenous economic power through AI:

- **Scale:** Building toward an "Indigenous World Economic Forum"
- **AI Integration:** Three core AI advisors, custom Indigenomics AI engine
- **Strategic Partnership:** Kris as narrative shaper for the movement
- **Timeline Acceleration:** Developments that seemed impossible a year ago now operational
- **Core Philosophy:** AI as a tool to activate humanity's best potential

### **Philippe Pasquier - Metacreation Lab Showcase**
**Academic-Industry-Artist Triangle**

Philippe demonstrated why the Vancouver AI community has become essential for bridging research and application:

- **Lab Legacy:** Operating since 2008, before generative AI was mainstream
- **Autodoom Tool:** Small-data model crafting for artists, zero-code training
- **Research Impact:** MIDI tokenizer downloaded 120,000 times
- **Industry Critique:** Big tech AI models biased toward Western perspectives
- **Artist Empowerment:** Direct manipulation tools vs. prompt dependency

**Collaborative Projects:**
- **Arabic Calligraphy Generation** (with Harsha Sobhan)
- **Interactive Face Morphing** (with Lionel and Kris's photo archive)
