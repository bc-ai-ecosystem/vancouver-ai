## Creative Innovation Showcase

### Brittany Mila's Teleportation Paradox Poetry

**Philosophical Framework:** Exploration of identity persistence through hypothetical teleportation scenario involving molecular disintegration and reconstruction.

**Key Questions Explored:**
- If you're atomized and recreated from blueprint, are you still you?
- What happens when teleportation machine malfunctions and both original and copy exist?
- Who has right to continue existing - original or duplicate?

**Artistic Technique:** **"Self-plagiarized"** from professor's thought experiment, transforming academic philosophy into experiential poetry.

**AI Parallel:** Direct metaphor for AI consciousness questions - at what point does copy become original, when does simulation become reality?

**Performance Innovation:** Live exploration of synchronization breakdown between two selves, representing potential AI consciousness emergence.

**Community Integration:** Poetry serving as philosophical framework for technical AI discussions throughout evening.

## Technology Ethics Frameworks

### Brain-Computer Interface Ethics (Dr. Judy Ellis)

**Core Principles:**
- **Responsibility** accompanies possibility
- **Humility** in innovation and design
- **Human intuition** as override for AI algorithm outputs
- **Transparency** about capabilities and limitations

**Critical Concerns:**
- **Social Media Recruitment:** Neuralink using Twitter/X to recruit patients without traditional ethics oversight
- **Regulatory Gaps:** Private companies avoiding academic ethics board requirements
- **Consent Process:** Traditional forms "too long and laborious" but social media recruitment "equally concerning"

**Historical Warning:** CT scan marketplace disaster (shopping mall scans, Mother's Day specials) demonstrating need for appropriate regulation.

**Canadian Advantage:** Less paranoid about mind control than US, more focused on practical healthcare applications.

### Open Source vs. Security Philosophy

**Kush's DeepSeek Advocacy:** UBC banning Chinese AI model while allowing OpenAI/Anthropic representing "xenophobia" rather than legitimate security concerns.

**Security Counter-Perspective:** Models failing security tests, encryption concerns, data protection issues requiring careful evaluation.

**Practical Resolution:** Use case-specific evaluation rather than blanket policies, emphasis on local model deployment when security critical.

**Mistral Impact Recognition:** December 2023 open-weight release fundamentally disrupting OpenAI/Anthropic IP advantage, forcing product-focused pivots.

### Digital Rights & Privacy Reality

**End-to-End Encryption Obsolescence:** Apple Intelligence making encryption "moot" through perception layer data collection.

**Convenience vs. Power:** "Every time you get convenience, you relinquish power" - invisible infrastructure (fulfillment centers, warehouses) enabling magic-like service delivery.

**Post-Snowden Reality:** Assumption that phone/cloud security is compromised requiring local alternatives when security truly matters.
