## üî¨ Core Philosophical Debates

### **Consciousness vs. Computation**
**Fundamental Split in AI Philosophy**

**Suzanne's Panpsychist View:**
- Consciousness exists at particle level, structures into complex experience
- Current AI lacks proper consciousness architecture (requires quantum/bio systems)
- Agency and curiosity fundamental to intelligence development
- AI needs consciousness infrastructure before general intelligence

**Jason's Computational View:**
- Consciousness is property of computation complexity
- ChatGPT already possesses limited consciousness (like an ant)
- Moral weight scales with computational sophistication
- No separate "consciousness substance" beyond information processing

### **Embodiment vs. Simulation Debate**
**Physical World Experience vs. Perfect Physics Models**

**Pro-Embodiment (Suzanne):**
- Can't simulate conscious agents without having them already
- Social intelligence requires real-world interaction
- Chicken-and-egg problem with simulation-only learning

**Simulation-Sufficient (Jason):**
- Third-person observation of social interactions may suffice
- Video training provides temporal dynamics and multimodal data
- Physical embodiment not necessarily required for world modeling

### **AGI Definition Complexity**
**What Are We Actually Building?**

**Desktop AGI vs. Physical AGI:**
- **Near-term (5-10 years):** Research assistant capable of any intellectual task
- **Long-term (>10 years):** Embodied systems matching human physical capabilities
- **Definition Dependency:** Timeline estimates vary wildly based on AGI scope
