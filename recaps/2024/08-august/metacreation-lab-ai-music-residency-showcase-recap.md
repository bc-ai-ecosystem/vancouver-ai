## ðŸ’¡ Technologies Showcased

### Calliope - Music GPT
- **What it does:** Generates musical scores (MIDI) trained on vast database of all music styles
- **Key feature:** Prompt with your own music to generate complementary tracks
- **Use case:** Game composers needing thousands of variations, musicians seeking inspiration

### Autolume - Neural Visual Synthesizer
- **What it does:** Allows artists to train models on their own visual work
- **Key feature:** Real-time control via physical controllers, no coding required
- **Use case:** Visual artists creating personalized AI models for their unique style

### MML Plugin for Cubase/Ableton
- **What it does:** Integrates AI generation directly into production software
- **Key feature:** Style transfer, variation generation, arrangement assistance
- **Use case:** Producers needing quick variations or breaking creative blocks
